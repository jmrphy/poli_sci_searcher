Conduct a basic Political Science literature review in 1 minute.
  • 1. Email your professor saying nevermind, you don't need help with your literature review.
  • 2. Set your browser to allow popup windows (or try a search and let it prompt you to allow popups).
  • 3. Enter your search term(s) in the box below and click "Search" (duh).
  • 4. Three new browser tabs will open showing relevant articles from proper political science journals.
  • 5. Go remove that line in your essay about how nothing has been written on your topic!
